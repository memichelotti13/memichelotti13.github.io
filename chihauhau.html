Chihauhua Lovers Anonymous

The first step is admittance.

If you like angry, little things that bite your toes, you've come to the right place.

  • Named after the state in Mexico.
  • Two types: long-haired and short-haired
  • Current breed standards defined by registries specify an "apple-head" or "apple-dome" skull conformation.
  • Chihuahuas have large, round eyes and large, erect ears, set in a high, dramatically rounded skull.
  • Many breeders try to breed Chihuahuas as small as possible, because those marketed as "teacup" or "tiny teacup" demand higher prices, which makes it hard to continue breeding them.
